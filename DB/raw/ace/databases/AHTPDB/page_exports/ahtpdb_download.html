DatasetDescription

Small Peptides (2 - 5 residues)

It contains about 4445 experimentally validated small Antihypertensive peptides which are 2-5 amino acids in length & collected and complied from literature, from various natural resources.

Long Peptides (6 - 16 residues)

It contains about 1533 experimentally validated Antihypertensive peptides which are of 6 to 16 or more in length size & collected and complied from literature, from various natural resources.

Peptides with IC50

It contains about 3364 experimentally validated Antihypertensive peptides which have IC50 (ACE inhibitory concentration) is given & which are collected and complied from literature, from various natural resources.
